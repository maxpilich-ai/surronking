FREQUENTLY ASKED

Everything you need to know before ordering
How long does delivery take?

Delivery takes 4-8 weeks depending on your location. Because we sell direct without a middleman, your bike ships straight to you — that's how we keep prices thousands below what dealers charge. You'll get tracking updates the whole way.

What's included with each bike?

Everything you need to ride — the bike, battery, charger, manual, and a free maintenance kit. Some models include additional accessories.

Is a license required to ride?

It depends on your location. In most areas you don't need a motorcycle license, but requirements vary. Check your local regulations before purchasing.

How long does the battery last?

These batteries are rated for 500+ charge cycles, which works out to about 2-3 years of regular riding. They hold their capacity well over time, and we carry replacement batteries if you ever need one.

What's your policy on delivery and responsibility?

Your bike is on us until it hits your door — we make sure it arrives perfect. Once you sign for it, it's yours to enjoy. Just give it a quick look-over when it shows up.

Can I customize or mod my bike?

Absolutely. The Surron community is big on customization. We carry parts and can point you to guides — it's your bike, make it yours.

What if something breaks after purchase?

Reach out to us and we'll help you troubleshoot or point you in the right direction. We also carry replacement parts at solid prices — we've got you covered.
